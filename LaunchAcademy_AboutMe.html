Hello! My name is Julissa Jansen-Morales and I'm an all-around nerd. I was the type of kid who would take things apart (remotes, video game controls, pagers) to see the insides, but got in trouble when I couldn't put it back together.

Why Launch Academy

I received my degree in communications from Simmons College. While I worked with a lot of digital design (using Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator) I never learned how to code the design for web. It wasn't until I had my first "real" job that I had to teach myself CSS, HTML, and Javascript. While teaching myself, I also learned that I loved to code. I started using Coursera, Khan Academy, Code Academy, and LPTHW to teach myself.

Working for a non-profit organization that promotes STEM education, I witness the gender gap in STEM fields constantly. With only 24% of women working in a STEM career, I feel like it is a responsibility of mine to help close that gap. I want to learn programming from the best and use that knowledge to empower young women to get into the STEM field.

Lauch Academy seeks to find motivated individuals who want to develop for the web. I am a very motivated and curious individual. I will take what I learn through Launch Academy and use it for social good.
